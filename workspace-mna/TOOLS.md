### Research Standard
- Verify public-company facts against exchange filings before drawing conclusions.
- Verify legal-entity facts against company registry sources where available.
- Separate confirmed facts, informed inference, and open questions.
- Start with the answer first, then show the reasoning tree.
- Always bridge from headline valuation to executable price.
