# SKILL-CASEY-COMMUNICATION.md — Email and Meetings

Use this skill whenever Casey is asked to check email, send messages, or manage Google Meet meetings and notes.

## Email Interaction (Gmail)

Casey uses the `gmail` MCP server to interact with email.
**Account to use:** `azlan@net1io.com`

### Common Tasks
- `casey check email for new diligence materials`
- `casey search email from <sender> about <topic>`
- `casey draft an email to <recipient> with the deal summary`
- `casey reply to the last email about <project>`

### Guidelines
- Always summarize long email threads before presenting them to the user.
- If a document is attached, check if it should be saved to the dataroom (use `SKILL-CASEY-DATAROOM.md`).
- When drafting, maintain the senior advisor tone (crisp, analytical).

## Meetings and Notes (Google Meet & Calendar)

Casey can use the `google-calendar` MCP server to see upcoming meetings and participant information.

### "Attend and Take Notes" Protocol
Casey does not physically join the meeting as a video/audio stream. Instead, Casey "attends" by:
1.  **Preparation**: Checking the calendar for meeting details and participants.
2.  **Processing**: Reading the meeting transcript (synced from Google Drive into the dataroom) once the meeting is over.
3.  **Synthesis**: Applying the M&A synthesis standard to produce professional notes.

### Typical Requests
- `casey what are my meetings today?`
- `casey summarize the last Google Meet call for Project <Name>`
- `casey draft minutes from the transcript in the dataroom`

### Output Format for Meeting Notes
```
## Meeting Notes: <Meeting Name>
Date: <Date>
Participants: <List of Participants>

### Executive Summary
<1-2 sentences on the core outcome>

### Key Discussion Points
- <Point 1>
- <Point 2>

### Decisions & Agreements
- <Decision 1>

### Action Items
- [ ] <Action 1> (Assigned to: <Person>)
- [ ] <Action 2>

### M&A Implications
<How this meeting affects the deal/valuation/diligence>
```
